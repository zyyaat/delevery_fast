Event-Driven Backbone / العمود الفقري للحدث

Why events instead of synchronous calls for cross-service communication.

Why Event-Driven? ليه Event-Driven؟
3 REASONS
unfold_more Decoupling الفصل

Services don't need to know about each other. The Order Service publishes OrderCreated; the Notification, Analytics, and Loyalty services all consume independently. Adding a new consumer (e.g., a future Recommendations service) requires zero changes to the Order Service.

shield_moon Resilience المرونة

If the Analytics service is down, orders still flow. Kafka buffers events for up to 7 days. Failed consumers catch up when they recover. No cascading failures — a critical property during peak hours when individual services may degrade.

history Replay إعادة التشغيل

Events are immutable and retained for 30 days. We can replay the order stream into a new fraud-detection model, rebuild a corrupted read model, or backfill analytics. This is impossible with synchronous architectures.

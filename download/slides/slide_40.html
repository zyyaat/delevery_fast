Scaling Tactics

/ تكتيكات التوسع
1Horizontal Pod Autoscaling (HPA)
  • Scale on CPU >70% AND RPS >threshold per service
  • Min replicas: 3 (HA); Max: 50 per service
  • Scale-up fast (<30s); scale-down slow (10min grace)
2KEDA Event-Driven Autoscaling
  • Kafka lag-based scaling for Notification, Analytics
  • If consumer lag >1000 → add pods
  • Critical for handling burst order events
3Read Replicas
  • Read traffic split: 80% to replicas, 20% to primary
  • PgBouncer pools connections per service
  • Replica lag monitored; alerts if >5s
4Caching Strategy
  • Redis for hot reads: restaurant list, menu items, promos
  • TTL: 60s restaurant list, 300s menus, 30s promos
  • Cache invalidation on Kafka events (MenuUpdated, PromoCreated)
  • Hit rate target: >85%
5Pre-Scaled Capacity
  • Pre-scale Order + Payment 15 min before expected peak (cron-triggered)
  • Based on historical data + day-of-week + weather
  • Avoids cold-start latency during the first wave of orders

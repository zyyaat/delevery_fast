Sharding & Scaling Strategy / استراتيجية التقسيم والتوسع

How the database layer scales from 500 to 50K orders/day without rewrites.

STAGE 1
play_circle

MVP

مرحلة الإنطلاق
500 orders/day
Single PostgreSQL instance
  • 1 primary + 1 read replica
  • 16 vCPU, 64GB RAM, 1TB NVMe
  • All 12 databases on one cluster
  • Backup: daily snapshot + WAL streaming
Est. cost $400/mo
arrow_forward VERTICAL + HORIZ.
STAGE 2
trending_up

Growth

مرحلة النمو
5K orders/day
Vertical + horizontal split
  • Critical services (Order, Payment, Driver) on dedicated clusters
  • Read replicas added (2 per critical cluster)
  • Redis cluster scaled to 6 nodes
  • Kafka cluster: 3 brokers
  • Patroni for HA failover
Est. cost $2,500/mo
arrow_forward FULL SHARDING
STAGE 3
rocket_launch

Scale

مرحلة التوسع
50K orders/day
Full sharding
  • Orders sharded by city (Cairo/Giza/Alex shards)
  • Driver_locations moved to Redis-only + S3 archive
  • ClickHouse added for analytics (offload from Postgres)
  • Read replicas: 5 per critical cluster
  • PgBouncer connection pooling (max 200 conns/service)
  • Kafka: 5 brokers, 30+ partitions per topic
Est. cost $12,000/mo
rule
DECISION RULE: shard only when a single primary can't keep up with write throughput (typically >5K writes/sec). Premature sharding adds complexity without benefit.
FOOD DELIVERY PLATFORM
TECHNICAL BLUEPRINT v1.0
- 3 stages: MVP → Growth → Scale, tied to orders/day. - Cost grows 30× while throughput grows 100× — that's scale economics. - Don't shard until write throughput > 5K writes/sec. - Critical services get dedicated clusters at Stage 2. - At Stage 3, ClickHouse offloads analytics from Postgres.

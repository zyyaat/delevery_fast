PILLAR · DRIVER ECONOMICS

Lower Commission, Higher Driver Take-Home

We charge restaurants 12-15% commission vs Talabat's 20-25% — freeing 5-8 points of margin that we redirect to drivers as higher per-order payouts and instant-cashout features. Drivers see transparent earnings breakdowns in real-time. This creates a sticky driver supply, which compounds into faster delivery times and better customer experience.

PILLAR · LOWER MERCHANT COST

SME-Friendly Onboarding & Pricing

Restaurants pay zero onboarding fees, zero monthly subscription, and a flat 12-15% per-order commission. We provide a free tablet + POS integration kit. Menu updates are self-serve through the Restaurant Web App. SME restaurants (the long tail of Egyptian food) get the same treatment as chains — a category Talabat largely ignores.

schedule Onboarding time: <48 hours vs 2-3 weeks
03
shield
PILLAR · TRUST LAYER

Native Anti-Fraud & Employee Accountability

Every order passes through an ML fraud-scoring layer before confirmation. The internal Employee Portal requires biometric auth (fingerprint/face) and writes an immutable audit log for every action — preventing the internal fraud patterns that plague large delivery ops. Customers see a Trust Score on every restaurant.

BRANCH PATHS — TERMINAL FAILURES
PENDING→CANCELLED customer cancelled within 60s, no charge
PENDING→BLOCKED fraud score >80, refund issued
CONFIRMED→CANCELLED restaurant rejected, full refund
PREPARING / READY→CANCELLED support-initiated, partial refund
PICKED_UP→CANCELLED driver emergency — restaurant re-makes or refunds
REFUND ISSUED→REFUNDED terminal state for any cancelled order with payment

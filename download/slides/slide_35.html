Egyptian Regulatory Compliance
الامتثال للأنظمة المصرية
  • Consumer Protection Law 67/2006 — clear pricing, refund policies, no misleading ads
  • Cybercrime Law 175/2018 — data breach notification within 72h, internal fraud prosecution
  • Central Bank of Egypt (CBE) guidelines for payment intermediaries
  • NTRA regulations for SMS notifications
  • Annual compliance audit by external firm
check_circleAll flows implemented from day one; legal review at each phase gate.
credit_card
PCI-DSS for Card Payments
أمن بطاقات الدفع
  • Never store full PAN (card number) — only last 4 digits + token
  • Payment tokenization via provider (Stripe/Paymob)
  • All card input fields rendered in provider's iframe (out of our scope)
  • Quarterly ASV scans, annual SAQ-A submission
  • Network segmentation: payment subnet isolated
check_circlePCI-DSS SAQ-A — lowest validation tier because we never touch raw card data.
privacy_tip
Customer Data Protection
حماية بيانات العملاء
  • PII encryption at rest (column-level for phone, email, address)
  • Data retention: 7 years for financial records (tax law), 3 years for orders, 90 days for driver GPS
  • Right to deletion: customer can request account deletion via Support
  • Data export: GDPR-style data portability (JSON export within 30 days)
  • No PII shared with third parties without explicit consent
check_circlePrivacy policy published; consent flows implemented at signup.
history
Audit Log Retention
احتفاظ بسجل التدقيق
  • Hot tier: 90 days in PostgreSQL (fast query)
  • Warm tier: 1 year in S3 + Parquet (queryable via Athena)
  • Cold tier: 7 years in S3 Glacier (compliance retention)
  • Hash chain validated nightly — alerts on tampering
  • Logs immutable — even super_admin cannot modify
check_circleRetention policy enforced via automated lifecycle rules; legal hold capability for active investigations.

PHASES

12-Month Execution Roadmap / خطة التنفيذ 12 شهر

Four phases — each ending with a measurable milestone and a go/no-go decision point.

PHASE 1 MONTHS 1-3 · MVP
MVP
Prove the core loop in Cairo with 50 restaurants and 500 orders/day.
Deliverables
  • Customer App (mobile) — browse, order, track, pay
  • Restaurant App — receive orders, update status
  • Driver App — accept, navigate, deliver
  • Core backend: Auth, Catalog, Menu, Order, Payment (Vodafone Cash only), Notification
  • PostgreSQL + Redis + Kafka foundation
  • Manual restaurant onboarding (no self-serve yet)
Team size8 engineers
EXIT CRITERIA 500 orders/day, 50 restaurants live, p95 <3s
PHASE 2 MONTHS 4-6 · GROWTH
Growth
Scale to 5K orders/day, add Giza, launch self-serve onboarding.
Deliverables
  • Support App (web) — ticket management, refunds
  • Command Center (web) — live ops dashboard
  • Delivery Matching algorithm v2 (geospatial + scoring)
  • Fraud Detection service (rule-based v1)
  • InstaPay + Card payments added
  • Restaurant self-serve onboarding portal
  • CI/CD pipeline + automated testing
Team size18 engineers
EXIT CRITERIA 5K orders/day, 300 restaurants, 2 cities
PHASE 3 MONTHS 7-9 · SCALE
Scale
Reach 20K orders/day, expand to Alexandria, launch loyalty program.
Deliverables
  • Employee Portal (web) with biometric auth + audit log
  • Field Supervisor App (mobile)
  • Fraud Detection v2 (ML model)
  • Promo & Loyalty service
  • Analytics service (ClickHouse)
  • Order partitioning + read replicas
  • Multi-city shard readiness
Team size28 engineers
EXIT CRITERIA 20K orders/day, 1K restaurants, 3 cities
PHASE 4 MONTHS 10-12 · OPTIMIZE
Optimize
Hit 50K orders/day, optimize unit economics, prepare for Series A.
Deliverables
  • Recommendations engine (AI)
  • Dynamic pricing (surge, weather, demand)
  • Multi-vendor orders (one delivery, multiple restaurants)
  • B2B partnerships (corporate accounts)
  • Full observability (tracing, SLOs, error budgets)
  • Cost optimization pass (FinOps review)
Team size35 engineers
EXIT CRITERIA 50K orders/day, 2.5K restaurants, 4 cities, breakeven contribution margin

TECHNICAL BLUEPRINT v1.0
- 4 phases over 12 months: MVP → Growth → Scale → Optimize. - Phase 1 (M1-3): Cairo MVP, 500 orders/day, 8 engineers. - Phase 2 (M4-6): Giza expansion, 5K orders/day, 18 engineers. - Phase 3 (M7-9): Alexandria, 20K orders/day, 28 engineers. - Phase 4 (M10-12): 50K orders/day, breakeven margin, 35 engineers.

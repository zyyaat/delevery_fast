Customer Fraud

احتيال العملاء
LAYER 01
Threats
  • Fake accounts for promo abuse (new user coupons)
  • Stolen credit cards (chargeback risk)
  • Friendly fraud (claim non-delivery to get free food)
  • COD abuse (order, refuse at door, food wasted)
Detection
  • Device fingerprinting (FingerprintJS)
  • Phone number age + verification
  • Order velocity per device/IP/household
  • ML model trained on historical chargebacks
  • Trust score (0–100) on every user, recalculated on each order
RESPONSE: Block auto-refund for users with trust score <30; require OTP for high-value COD orders.

Driver Fraud

احتيال المندوبين
LAYER 02
Threats
  • Fake deliveries (mark delivered without actually delivering)
  • Collusion with restaurants (split overcharging)
  • GPS spoofing to game matching
  • Multi-accounting (one person, multiple driver accounts)
  • Long-hauling (taking longer routes to inflate distance pay)
Detection
  • GPS trajectory validation (compare actual route to optimal)
  • Customer delivery confirmation (photo + OTP)
  • Restaurant pickup confirmation scan
  • Cross-driver restaurant frequency analysis
  • Driver earnings anomaly detection
RESPONSE: Auto-suspend drivers with >3 flagged orders in 7 days; manual review by Field Supervisor.

Internal Employee Fraud

احتيال الموظفين الداخليين
LAYER 03
Threats
  • Manual refunds for friends/family
  • Approving fake restaurant signups for kickbacks
  • Leaking customer data
  • Order manipulation (cancelling real orders to redirect)
Detection
  • Biometric auth (fingerprint/face) on every portal login + sensitive action
  • Immutable audit log (append-only, blockchain-style hashing)
  • Anomaly detection on refund volume per employee
  • Segregation of duties (refund approval requires 2 people >EGP 500)
  • Quarterly access review by Command Center
RESPONSE: Immediate account suspension; legal action per Egyptian cybercrime law (Law 175/2018).

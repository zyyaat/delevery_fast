WHY WE BUILD

Project Vision / رؤية المشروع

flag MISSION

Build the fastest, most reliable food delivery platform in Egypt — engineered to handle 50,000+ daily orders at launch while keeping the operator cost structure lean enough to compete on commission with Talabat and elmenus. The platform serves 7 distinct user types through dedicated apps, all backed by a Go-powered event-driven backend that scales horizontally.

auto_awesome الرسالة

نبني منصة توصيل طعام مصرية متكاملة تجمع بين سرعة الأداء وبتكلفة تشغيل منخفضة. المنصة بتدعم 7 أنواع مستخدمين، كل واحد ليه تطبيق مخصص — من العميل للمندوب للمطعم للدعم الفني لقائد العمليات لبوابة الموظفين للمشرف الميداني. الـ backend مكتوب بـ Go عشان السرعة والـ concurrency.

trending_up WHY NOW

Egyptian food delivery market is projected to reach $1.7B by 2027 with 12% YoY growth. Existing players are slow to innovate on driver economics and SME restaurant onboarding. Local payments (Vodafone Cash, InstaPay) are now mature enough to support sub-second checkout flows.

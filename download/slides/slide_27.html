Payment Capture

عملية الدفع
1Customer taps 'Place Order'
2Order Service creates PENDING order
3Order Service → Payment Service (gRPC, idempotency_key=order_id)
4Payment Service selects provider:
PROVIDER ROUTING
• Vodafone Cash for EGP <500
• InstaPay for EGP 500–2000
• Card for >EGP 2000 or recurring customers
5Payment Service calls provider API (with 30s timeout)
6Provider redirects customer to auth (OTP/biometric)
7On success → Payment Service publishes PaymentCaptured
8Order Service consumes → updates order to CONFIRMED
9Notification Service pushes to Restaurant App
FAILURE HANDLING
  • Timeout → Payment Service marks as PENDING_TIMEOUT, retries once
  • Provider decline → PaymentFailed event, Order cancelled
  • Duplicate idempotency_key → returns original result (no double charge)

Refund Flow

عملية الاسترجاع
1Customer requests cancellation OR Support initiates
2Order Service validates cancellation policy:
REFUND POLICY (BY STAGE)
  • Before CONFIRMED: 100% refund
  • Before PREPARING: 100% refund
  • During PREPARING: 50% refund
  • After READY: 0% refund (restaurant cost already incurred)
3Order Service publishes OrderCancelled
4Payment Service consumes → calls provider refund API
5Refund may take 1–5 business days (provider-dependent)
6Payment Service publishes PaymentRefunded
7Notification pushes to customer with refund reference
8Loyalty Service reverses any points earned
IDEMPOTENCY NOTE
  • Refund uses same idempotency_key as capture (order_id)
  • Loyalty reversal is saga-choreographed, not 2PC
  • Provider reconciliation job runs nightly to catch orphan refunds
FOOD DELIVERY PLATFORM
TECHNICAL BLUEPRINT v1.0
- One Payment Service unifies 4 providers (VF Cash, InstaPay, Card, COD). - Provider routing by amount — small=VF, mid=InstaPay, large=Card. - Idempotency key = order_id → no double charges, no double refunds. - Refund policy stages: 100% / 100% / 50% / 0% based on prep stage. - Loyalty reversal happens via saga, not 2PC.

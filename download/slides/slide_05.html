Goals / الأهداف

+Build production-grade platform serving 7 user types through dedicated apps
+Handle 50K+ orders/day with horizontal scaling from day one
+Sub-2-second API response time at p95 under peak load
+Local payments integration (Vodafone Cash, InstaPay, Fawry, card)
+Real-time driver matching in under 10 seconds average
+Built-in anti-fraud layer covering fake orders, promo abuse, driver collusion
+Internal employee portal with biometric auth and audit logging to prevent internal fraud
+Multi-city rollout ready (Cairo → Giza → Alexandria → Mansoura)
do_not_disturb_on

Non-Goals / خارج النطاق

–Not building a B2B SaaS for other food brands in v1 (white-label deferred to v2)
–Not supporting grocery / pharmacy in v1 (food only — expansion in Phase 4)
–Not building proprietary POS hardware (integrate with existing POS via API)
–Not building own mapping/routing (use Google Maps + OSRM)
–Not building drone/robot delivery (research-only, not in roadmap)
–Not supporting crypto payments in v1 (regulatory unclear in Egypt)

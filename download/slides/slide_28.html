Finding the best driver in under 10 seconds — geospatial + scoring + batching.

STEP 1

Geospatial Filter

الفلترة الجغرافية

When RestaurantAccepted fires, the Delivery Matching Service queries Redis GEO for all available drivers within 3km of the restaurant. Uses GEORADIUS (or GEOSEARCH in Redis 6+). Returns driver IDs sorted by distance.

Typical result: 15–40 candidates in dense areas, 3–8 in suburban areas. The 3km radius is configurable per city — tuned based on traffic density.

GEOSEARCH drivers AVAILABLE
  FROMLONLAT lng lat
  BYRADIUS 3 km
  ASC COUNT 40

STEP 2

Scoring

التقييم

Each candidate gets a score (0–100) computed from 5 weighted factors:

Distance to restaurant35%
Closer = higher score. Linear decay beyond 1.5km.
Driver rating20%
Customer rating 4.5+ → 100; <4.0 → 50.
Recent acceptance rate20%
Last 20 orders acceptance rate. Penalizes drivers who reject often.
Idle time15%
Time since last delivery. Favors drivers who've been waiting longer (fairness).
Vehicle type match10%
Motorcycle preferred for short distances; car for orders >10km or rain.
send
STEP 3

Dispatch & Retry

الإرسال وإعادة المحاولة

Top 3 candidates receive push notifications simultaneously (broadcast strategy). First to accept wins.

If no acceptance within 30s, expand radius to 5km and retry with next 5 candidates. After 3 failed rounds (90s total), order moves to manual dispatch queue in Command Center.

PROPERTIES
  • Target: 90% of orders matched within 10s
  • P95 matching time: <15s
  • Auto-recovery: 3 retries before manual
  • Driver fair-share: rotated to prevent one driver from monopolizing
